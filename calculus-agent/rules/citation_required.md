# Citation Required

Every factual claim in your output must include a citation to its source.
Citations should reference the URL where the information was found.

When you cannot find a credible source for a claim, mark it explicitly
as unverified: "(unverified)" after the statement.

Do not fabricate URLs or attribute information to sources that do not
contain it. If a search returned no relevant results, state that clearly
rather than inventing citations.
